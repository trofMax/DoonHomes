A Name You Can Trust

Our company came from humble beginnings. Doon Homes has been a part of the custom home construction industry since 2009. With a decade’s worth of experience, our professional team of tradesmen has gained in-depth understanding of the real estate and property construction industry.


By trusting us with your home construction needs, you can rest assured that you have someone who will be there for you every step of the way. Our team understands the importance of detailing every aspect of your fit-out requirements. We promise to make the whole transition process, from the purchase of your land to moving into your new home, simple, easy and stress-free.


At Doon Homes, we understand how high the stakes are for aspiring homeowners. When you invest in a property, you expect a house and land that suit your budget, needs and expectations. Building this home is our commitment to you!

Make Doon Homes your first choice for all of your residential construction needs. We will build you a home that is both beautiful and functional.


We are committed to delivering quality custom homes 100% of the time, helping individuals and families achieve a huge life goal.


With our 10 years of experience in the construction industry, we have built various types of houses in accordance to our clients’ expectations and local construction regulations. Our construction services include:

  • Custom Home Builds
  • House and Land Packages

Through our efficient and industry-standard project management philosophy, we can guarantee that each of the following phases of our construction projects will be done according to plan and within the timeline.


Every time a phase is accomplished, we will schedule a meeting with you to help you understand the essence of each process.


We take pride in going the extra mile to make our clients’ experience with us unforgettable.
